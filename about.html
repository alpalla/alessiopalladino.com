About

Hi! I'm Alessio. I'm a software engineer with a background in computer science. I'm passionate about building impactful products, as well as building a company culture that fosters personal and technical development.

I became a software engineer because I'm curious about how things work and I love building things. I loved Legos as a kid, and I still love them today. The feeling of slowly putting pieces together to make something bigger than yourself is a feeling I have been chasing all my life. While I may not feel this childlike wonder every workday, I've kept it as my own personal North Star throughout my software engineering career.

I'm currently working as a Senior Frontend Engineer. In my day to day I work with TypeScript, React, Next.js, and React Native to deliver multiple Web, iOS, and Android applications. From time to time I contribute to open source code and write technical articles.

This website is a place I created for myself to write about what I know to learn it better. Feel free to check out my latest articles.
